Welcome To Our Resturant

Where Everything Tastes So-o-o Good



Dizzy Whizz has been serving the famous Whizzburger in Old Louisville for more than 70 years and still retains some traditions from the 1940s and 1950s with curbside service and counter seating.


Image via Burgers, Barbecue and Everything Else
https://burgersbarbecueandeverythingelse.com/2013/05/05/dizzy-whiz-louisville-ky/

In addition to fast breakfast meals, burgers, sandwiches and soups, there is a dairy bar that offers shakes, sundaes, banana splits, novelty ice cream treats and pies.
